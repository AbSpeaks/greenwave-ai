Gridlock drains hundreds of billions globally per year in degraded commercial productivity, wasted transport fuels, and logistical supply chain delays. Environmental Overhead

The stop-start cycle imposed by traditional traffic control is a primary catalytic amplifier of urban emissions. Harmonizing flows eliminates the source, not the symptom.
