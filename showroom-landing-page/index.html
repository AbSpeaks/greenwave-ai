The Context

Indian Cities Are Losing the Battle Against Gridlock

Daily traffic jams in Pune, Mumbai, Bengaluru and other major cities waste time, fuel, and productivity. Traditional traffic lights run on outdated timers or reactive sensors. Hardware-based solutions are expensive, slow to deploy, and difficult to scale.

The Technology

A Better Way: Pure Software Intelligence

GreenWave AI models traffic as a continuous physics-based system. By analyzing live GPS telemetry, our engine forecasts congestion 10-15 minutes in advance and proactively optimizes signal timings.

Zero heavy hardware dependency

Integrates directly with existing GPS streams, eliminating the need for costly street-level installations.

Cloud-native and scalable

Orchestrated centrally on modern cloud architecture, allowing simultaneous optimization of adjacent intersections.

Faster & cost-effective deployment

Rolls out city-wide in weeks instead of years of construction work and hardware lock-in.

Designed for real Indian city conditions

Account for mixed vehicle flows, unlane-disciplined driving patterns, and sudden bottlenecks.

Our Mission

At GreenWave AI, we believe traffic congestion is a solvable engineering problem.

Our mission is to build the most intelligent traffic optimization platform for Indian cities — replacing expensive hardware with a lean, cloud-native software brain.

We want to save millions of hours, reduce pollution, improve safety, and make cities more livable.

Current Stage: Simulation & Validation

We are currently running high-fidelity simulation trials on coordinate models of actual Indian junctions. The platform is actively generating optimization matrices using real-world traffic flows, laying the engineering groundwork for physical pilot deployments.
